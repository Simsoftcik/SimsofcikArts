Art styles

I'm big fan of realistic and semi-realistic works and that's why I spend the most time on them. I also like to combine them with abstract elements. Another one of my styles is the animated one due to my interest in anime. Somethimes I merge it with realism and in this way I developed the style of the illustrations I made for the book I've written.

Software

When doing digital art, I usually use Krita

It's application to make from scratch and edit raster graphics. It's part of the Calligra suite, developed by the KDE community.
